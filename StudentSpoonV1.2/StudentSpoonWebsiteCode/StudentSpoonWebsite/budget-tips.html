Practical advice

Four habits that save money and reduce waste

📝

Plan before you shop

Check your cupboards first, choose meals for a few days and write a list. This reduces impulse buying and duplicate ingredients.

🍱

Cook extra portions

Make more than one serving when you can. Freeze or refrigerate the rest for a quick meal after college, work or placement.

🛒

Compare own-brand options

Own-brand staples such as rice, pasta, beans and frozen vegetables can reduce a meal cost without making the meal less useful.

↻

Give leftovers a new job

Use leftover vegetables in noodles, rice or a wrap. Small amounts of cooked food can become the base of another meal.
